Meet Enzo

Enzo is a very adorable high energy 4-months-old puppy. I got him when he was only 9 weeks old and weighed 20lbs. He is currently 63lbs, and thinks he is a lap dog. He loves to play with Diesel and at times sounds like a piglet. He also loves to play fetch, eat treats and go for walks.
