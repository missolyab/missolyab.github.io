About Pitbulls

Pitbull Type Height Distinct Characterictics
Pocket Males: under 17” at the withers, Females: under 14” at the withers A variation of the basic Standard Bully. The Pocket Bully has the same breed type, build and body type of the Standard Bully, but it is a bit shorter when it comes to height.
Classic Males: 17” – 20” at that withers, Females: 16” – 19” at the withers A variation of the basic Standard Bully. Its build and narrow body structure are what defines the dog. It has body frames that are light with less overall body mass regardless of what its gender is. It still possesses the usual Bully traits despite having a traditional Pit Bull look.
Standard Males: 17” – 20” at the withers, Females: 16” – 19” at the withers This dog is compact and medium or large. It has a blocky head and body that is muscular. The Bully gives an impression of high strength proportional to it size. It also has the build and looks that look bulky coupled with a substantial bone structure
Extreme This class has no appropriate height or weight Extreme Bully possesses more body mass along with body frame that tends to be heavier. The dog’s defining characteristics are having a wider look and more winkling.
XL Males: over 20” – 23” at the withers, Females: over 19” – 22” at the withers Expect the same breed type, body type and build in this dog. This dog is another amendment of the Standard American Bully. The difference between XL Bully and the Standard Bully is that the XL Bully is taller and is more muscular, more massive and bulkier.
